# Spiritual Mercury

A kinetic sculpture imagining a futuristic energy artifact, where reflective fluid forms rotate within a custom hubless ring structure. More at [dokidesigns.com/spiritualmercury](https://dokidesigns.com/spiritualmercury).

## Concept

Spiritual Mercury draws from digital mysticism and cybersigillism — a design language merging occult visual geometry with digital fabrication. The organic forms were developed in Blender using subdivision modifiers, and the rotation of the reflective surfaces is central to the work's meaning.

## How It Works

The engineering challenge was spinning an outer gear while keeping the center stationary. The solution uses a repurposed metal bed frame bar as an internal skeleton, mounted with wheels that grip the gear's track. A NEMA17 stepper motor drives the outer ring while the inner axis stays completely fixed — so contents inside remain motionless relative to the spinning exterior.
